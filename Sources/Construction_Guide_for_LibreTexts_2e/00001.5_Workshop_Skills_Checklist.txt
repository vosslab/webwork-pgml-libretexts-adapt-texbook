Oibretexts:

1.5: Workshop Skills Checklist

While the basic principles of editing and creating content on the
LibreTexts libraries are limited, there are several skills need to
master before creating and curating content. The Construction
Guide and Construction Forum are great resources to consult in
building these skills:

e The Construction Forum
e The Construction Guide

A recent Libretexts overview video is available too:

youtu.be/sxLmeHMegvTg.

Activity 1

e When your instructor's account is verified go to the
Conductor (https://commons.libretexts.org/home).

Activity 2

e Set up a Project in the Conductor

e Build a rudimentary book shell (one click, two entries)

Activity 3

e Go to the Remixer and build a few chapters/pages in your
new book

e Save the book set up in the Remixer to the
Library/Workbench area and go to it

Activity 4

e Rename Page (standard is "number: Title")

e Edit page (new or blank page and edit an existing page)
e Copy/past Existing Text

e Make hyperlink

e Add Box via templates
e Add equation

e Add video

e Add table

Activity 5

e Transclude (reuse) an existing page

Activity 6 (Advance)

e Add interactive component (via transclusion)
e Add Executable code (e.g. Python, R Sagemath)

Activity 7
e Add Copyright to a page

Activity 8
e Add Authorbar to a page

Activity 9
e Watch a page (little star next to social media buttons)

e Rate a Page
e Provide Feedback

Activity 10
e Export PDF
