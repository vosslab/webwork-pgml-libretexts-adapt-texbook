QTi - Fill-in-the-blank

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/qti---fill-in-the-blank
Last updated at 09/08/2025 11:38 AM by Delmar Larsen

Description: QTI Fill-in-the-Blank is a standardized question format where learners supply missing words, phrases, or values within a sentence or statement, allowing for precise or pattern-based answer matching.

Create a question with fill in the blanks by underlining the correct responses. Example. A stitch in time saves nine. If you would like to accept multiple answers, separate them with a vertical bar: "|". Example. January|February is a month that comes before March.
