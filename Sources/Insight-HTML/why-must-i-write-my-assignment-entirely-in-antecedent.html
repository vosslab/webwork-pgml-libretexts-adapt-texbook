Description: Why must I write my assignment entirely in The Forge?

Antecedent provides information about how you write your assignment to your instructor, including drafting, editing, and time information. Thus, it is essential that you write your assignment from beginning to end in the Antecedent word processor. You cannot, for example, write the assignment out with pen and paper and then type it up afterward. Further, you should ask your instructor before you use speech-to-text applications.
