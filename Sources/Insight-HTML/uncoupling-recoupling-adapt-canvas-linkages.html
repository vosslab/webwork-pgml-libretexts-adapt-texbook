Uncoupling and Coupling ADAPT courses to Canvas

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/uncoupling-recoupling-adapt-canvas-linkages
Last updated at 11/26/2025 01:57 AM by Jake Turner

Description: About uncoupling and coupling ADAPT courses to Canvas

Question: What happens if I unlink an existing Canvas course from ADAPT and then relink it (all API)? 

 

Answer: If the assignments are already in Canvas (with same name) the course will simply relink to ADAPT without changes.
