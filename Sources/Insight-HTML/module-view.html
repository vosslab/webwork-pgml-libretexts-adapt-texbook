ADAPT is synced to my Canvas LMS, but I do not see it online?

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/module-view
Last updated at 10/15/2025 01:49 PM by Delmar Larsen

Description: module view

If you cannot see the ADAPT assignments in your Canvas LMS after syncing you might be looking in the Module view instead of the assignments view. The LTI integration adds ADAPT assignments into the latter.

Remember that you need to "publish" the assignments so that students can see them.
