Description: Overview of the Students page on the Course Properties section of the ADAPT homework platform

The Course Properties section of ADAPT allows instructors to configure the core settings of their course. From here, they can adjust general course information, manage sections, control access through domain whitelisting, oversee student enrollment via the roster, and set other structural or administrative options. This area provides the foundational controls that define how the course operates, who can join, and how students are organized.
