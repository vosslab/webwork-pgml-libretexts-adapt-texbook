Description: How to address poor internet connection in The Forge

Some students using poor Wi-Fi signals may report that the word processor is a bit laggy. In general, students can expect a smooth writing experience if they have an internet connection greater than or equal to 3 Mbps, which is slower than almost all Wi-Fi signals. It is important that students write the assignment from beginning to end in the Antecedent word processor. Accordingly, you may advise students experiencing lags while typing to try the following: 

  1. Find a stronger Wi-Fi connection.
  2. Reload the page. 
  3. Try using Antecedent with a different device. 
  4. Create a personal hotspot with their phone and connect their laptop to their personal hotspot. (In most areas, personal hotspots are well above the required 3 Mbps.)
  5. Wait. Oftentimes, the lags while typing in the word processor will resolve themselves.

Many problems in the word processor can be resolved by reloading the page.
