It looks like we're having an issue connecting to ADAPT from your LMS because cookies are turned off. Cookies are small files that websites use to know if you're signed in. Below are some troubleshooting tips to try.

Turn on cookies in your browser:

Chrome

  1. Open Chrome and click the three dots (top right).
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Click Privacy and security.
  4. Select Cookies and other site data.
  5. Choose Allow all cookies.

Firefox

  1. Open Firefox and click the three lines (top right).
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Click Privacy & Security.
  4. Under Enhanced Tracking Protection, choose Standard.

Safari (Mac)

  1. Open Safari and click Safari in the menu bar.
  2. Select Settings (or Preferences).
  3. Go to the Privacy tab.
  4. Make sure Block all cookies is unchecked.

Edge

  1. Open Edge and click the three dots (top right).
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Click Cookies and site permissions.
  4. Select Manage and delete cookies and site data.
  5. Turn on Allow sites to save and read cookie data.

Clear your browser's history (cache and cookies), then try again:

Chrome

  1. Click the three dots -> History -> Clear browsing data.
  2. Choose All time.
  3. Check Cookies and other site data and Cached images and files.
  4. Click Clear data.

Firefox

  1. Click the three lines -> History -> Clear recent history.
  2. Set Time range to Everything.
  3. Check Cookies and Cache.
  4. Click OK.

Safari (Mac)

  1. Click Safari in the menu bar.
  2. Select Clear History.
  3. Choose all history and click Clear History.

Edge

  1. Click the three dots -> Settings.
  2. Click Privacy, search, and services.
  3. Under Clear browsing data, click Choose what to clear.
  4. Check Cookies and other site data and Cached images and files.
  5. Click Clear now.

Try a different browser (like Chrome or Firefox) if the issue continues.

Submit a ticket through our support center at https://support.libretexts.org if you still cannot connect.
