Description: How do I make comments on student assignments in The Forge?

  • You can directly edit language in students' assignments, as well as write comments directly on student assignments. By default, instructors open documents with track changes / record on. 
  • To make comments on student assignments, right click and select "Insert Comment."

Optional:  Click on the Full screen button to bring up full screen mode.
