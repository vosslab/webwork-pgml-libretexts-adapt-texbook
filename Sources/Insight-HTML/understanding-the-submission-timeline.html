Description: Understanding the Submission Timeline

The Submission Timeline shows information about the submission of the assignment and its various drafts. Students and instructors make all edits and comments on the document in the current Ongoing version of the document, indicated by the lavender running icon. While instructors are able to view previous versions of the assignment, they cannot edit or comment upon them (as they are in read-only mode).  Instructors can select which version of the assignment they wish to view by selecting the icon on the Submission Timeline. The version of the document currently shown to the instructor has a larger icon.

  • The Drafts rail at the top shows the time periods for the various drafts of the assignment, indicated by the thick teal line. 
  • The various submissions of the assignment by the student are indicated by the upward pointing arrows on the Submissions rail. On-time submissions by the student are indicated in green. Late submissions or instructor submissions are in orange.
  • Versions of the various submissions with instructor comments are indicated on the Comments rail.
