Linking Multiple Instructor Accounts in ADAPT

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/accountlinking
Last updated at 01/24/2025 02:58 PM by Delmar Larsen

Description: Linking Accounts

You can link up two or more ADAPT accounts together so that if you sign into one, you will have access to the others in a similar way that google does it (upper right pull down menu).

To do this sign into either of your existing ADAPT accounts and click your name in upper right. Click "settings"

In the new page under the title you will see "Linked Accounts". Click that

In the new page, write the email address of the new ADAPT account that you want linked to the one that you signed in as.


 ADAPT will send you a code with a 10-minute lifespan with a link to add it. 
 

That's it. You should then have two accounts (or more if you do this multiple times) that you can switch from one to another right next to your name in upper right.
