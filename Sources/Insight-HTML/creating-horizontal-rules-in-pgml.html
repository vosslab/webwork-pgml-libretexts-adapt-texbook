Creating Horizontal Rules in PGML

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/creating-horizontal-rules-in-pgml
Last updated at 06/04/2024 02:02 AM by Delmar Larsen

Description: https://webwork.maa.org/wiki/Rules_-_PGML

Three or more dashes or equals on a line by itself forms a rule

 -----

Normally, the rule will extend to fill the horizontal space used by the problem, but you can specify the width and size (i.e., height) if you want:

 ----{200px}
 ----{50%}
 ===={200px}{5px}
 ===={size=>'5px'}
 ----{width=>'200px',size=>'5px'}

You can center and right-justify rules:

 >> ----{100px} <<
 >> ----{100px}

They can also be indented.
