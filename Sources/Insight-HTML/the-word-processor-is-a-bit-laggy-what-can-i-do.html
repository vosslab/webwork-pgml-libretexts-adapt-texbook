Description: The word processor is a bit laggy. What can I do?

  1. Find a stronger Wi-Fi connection.
  2. Reload the page. (This can solve many performance issues.)
  3. Try using Antecedent with a different device. 
  4. Create a personal hotspot with your phone and connect your laptop to your personal hotspot. (In most areas, personal hotspots are well above the required 3 Mbps for a smooth writing experience.)
  5. Wait. Oftentimes, the lags while typing in the word processor will resolve themselves.
