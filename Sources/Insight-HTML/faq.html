Sharing ADAPT courses with Colleagues

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/faq
Last updated at 04/23/2025 07:13 PM by Justin Shorb

Description: What would be the easiest way to share a course with a colleague so that he/she could reused my assignments or least some of the problems?

First, you need to make the ADAPT course "public" under "general Information" of your course

Then others can "import" the course or view the course under "Public Courses" in the Dashboard pulldown menu.
