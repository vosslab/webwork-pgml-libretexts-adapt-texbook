You can compile the texts via the PDF button (although we will likely move it to the conductor page for the project; we are slowly migrating most curation capabilities to the Conductor and leaving the libraries mostly for hosted content). See image below.
Compile full recreates the PDFs for all pages and just Compile book is more of an "update PDF" that compiled only those pages that have been changed since the last compile. This is faster than full.
