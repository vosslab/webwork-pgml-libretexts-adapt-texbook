The concept of PG (Problem Generation) involves creating dynamic, algorithmic, and customizable questions that can provide instant feedback and adapt to different student responses.

The acronym "PG" for "Problem Generation" is specific to the context of WeBWorK and is not widely used outside of it. In WeBWorK, "PG" refers to the scripting language and system used to create and manage problems.
