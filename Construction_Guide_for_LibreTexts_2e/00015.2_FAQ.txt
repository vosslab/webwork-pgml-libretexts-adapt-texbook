How to access Spanish punctuation on my keyboard

For Windows users:

e Step 1-Click on the Start button and then gear shaped icon to Open settings.
e Step 2-Click on Time & Language.

e Step 3-Click on Language from the left menu.

e Step 4-Click on English (United states) to expand and then click on Options.
e Step 5 - Scroll down and then click on + Sign beside Add a language.

e Step 6- Select United states - International from the list.

e Step 7 - Now, in the Taskbar , click on your language .

e Step 8 - Now, select United states - International from the list.

For step by step instruction please see the following article.

If you follow all the steps that you have the US International keyboard set then,

Make sure it is turned on (look at the bottom right side corner of your screen and you should see ENG INTL)
Use the right side ALT key and then the vowel and it should put the accent on the vowel
