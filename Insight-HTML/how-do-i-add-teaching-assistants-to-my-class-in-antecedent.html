Description: How do I add Teaching Assistants to my class in The Forge?

  1. Once you are logged in, click on the My Classes (Instructor) button.
  2. Click on the name of the class.
  3. Click on the Teaching Assistants tab.
  4. Search for Teaching Assistants with their email address. They must already have an Antecedent account.

Click on the Add button.
