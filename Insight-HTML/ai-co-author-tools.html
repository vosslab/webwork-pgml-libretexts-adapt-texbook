Description: Learn more about using AI-enhanced authoring tools in Conductor

Introduction

Conductor's AI Co-Author tools are designed to provide OER content creators with an enhanced editing and curation experience. Current tools include AI-generation of:

  • Page summaries
  • Page tags (keywords)
  • Image alt text

In addition to AI-generation, Co-Author also provides a powerful interface for manually editing page summaries and tags. We'll dive deeper into using each of these tools below.

Reminder: While AI has become increasingly powerful, it's not always accurate. Thoroughly review all content before publishing.

 

Accessing Co-Author

Co-Author is available for all projects with connected books in Conductor. In your Conductor project's page, you'll see the pink “AI Co-Author” button in the top action bar.

 

Click there, and you'll be taken to the Co-Author page.

 

If you don't see the “AI Co-Author” button on your project's page, and believe you have a book connected, reach out to our Support Center and we will investigate.

 

Bulk Actions

In the top-right corner of the page, you'll see a “Bulk Actions” button. Click there to view your options.

Generate AI Metadata

Click “Generate AI Metadata” in the bulk actions dropdown. In the dialog that appears, you'll have the option to choose which types of metadata (summaries, tags, alt text) you want to generate, and if existing values should be overwritten.

Make your selections, and click “Generate” when ready. Because this process is resource-intensive, a bulk update job will be created and editing will be unavailable until that job has completed. Most bulk updates will complete within 4-6 minutes, though this will vary based on the types of metadata you're generating and the length of your content. You'll be sent an email when the update is complete.

 

 

Adding Tags in Bulk

The second option in the Bulk Actions dropdown, “Add Tags" allows you to easily apply multiple tags to multiple pages with just a few clicks.

Enter the tags you want to add, select the pages to add them to, and click “Save”. This will add the tags to your working changes, where you can continue to refine before saving.

 

View Bulk Update History

This option will allow you to see a history of the bulk update jobs that ran on your textbook. This is mainly for informational purposes only, and there's no need to complete any actions here.

 

Manual Editing

In addition to bulk generation of metadata, you can also create your own and/or refine what a bulk update job generated. Towards the bottom of the page, you'll see the table of contents of your textbook.

 

Click on the left arrow next to any entry to expand it.

 

You'll find entry for each page where you can edit the page summary, manage tags, and/or ask AI to re-generate them.

Removing Tags: Each tag has both an “X” icon and a “trash" icon. Click on the “X” to remove the tag from that page only. Click the “trash” icon to remove it from all pages. (you'll be prompted to confirm this before doing so)

 

When you're finished making your changes, click the “Save Changes” button and confirm your changes.
