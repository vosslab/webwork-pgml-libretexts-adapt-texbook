Description: Interfacing ADAPT to LMS

ADAPT connects to Learning Management Systems (LMSs) through the Learning Tools Interoperability (LTI) standard, which enables single sign-on, secure data exchange, and grade synchronization. Instructors link ADAPT problem sets or assessments directly into LMS courses as deep links, allowing students to launch assignments in ADAPT without a separate login. When students complete work in ADAPT, scores are returned to the LMS gradebook.

Canvas

ADAPT can connect to Canvas in two ways: through LTI alone or through a combined LTI-API (Application Programming Interface) setup. The combined option is recommended, as it streamlines the process by automatically generating and linking Modules, resulting in a smoother experience.
