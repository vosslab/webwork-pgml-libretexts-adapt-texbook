QTI - True/False

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/qti---truefalse
Last updated at 09/08/2025 11:36 AM by Delmar Larsen

Description: QTI True/False is a standardized question format that defines simple binary assessment items where learners choose between two options—true or false—for interoperability across platforms.

Write a question prompt and then create a selection of answers, choosing the correct answer from the list. Students will receive a shuffled ordering of the selection. Optionally provide feedback at the individual question level or general feedback for a correct response, an incorrect response, or any response.
