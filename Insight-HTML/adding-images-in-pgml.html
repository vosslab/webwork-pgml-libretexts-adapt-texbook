Adding Images in PGML

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/adding-images-in-pgml
Last updated at 06/04/2024 02:01 AM by Delmar Larsen

Description: https://webwork.maa.org/wiki/Command_substitution_-_PGML

PGML doesn't yet include any direct support for images, but you can get them as in the following example

 [@ image(insertGraph($graph), width=>300, height=>300) @]*
