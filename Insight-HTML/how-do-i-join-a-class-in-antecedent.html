Description: How do I join a class in The Forge?

  1. Once you are logged in, click on the My Classes (Student) button.
  2. Click on the Enrollment Key* field. 
  3. Type the enrollment key given to you by your instructor (e.g., AB-1X24G).
  4. Select the magnifying glass.
  5. Once you have found the correct class, click Enroll.
