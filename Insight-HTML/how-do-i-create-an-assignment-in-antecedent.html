Description: How do I create an assignment in The Forge?

  1. Once you are logged in, click on the My Classes (Instructor) button.
  2. Click on the name of the class for which you wish to create an assignment.
  3. Click on the Assignments tab. 
  4. Click on the Add Assignment + button. 
  5. Enter the relevant information for the assignment.

Click on the Create button.
