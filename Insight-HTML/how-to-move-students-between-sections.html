How to Move Students Between Sections

Source: https://commons.libretexts.org/insight/how-to-move-students-between-sections
Last updated at 08/25/2025 03:32 PM by Jake Turner

Description: Follow the tutorial below to move students that may have enrolled in the wrong section of your ADAPT course.

1. From the "My Courses" page (https://adapt.libretexts.org/instructor/courses) Click the "gear" icon next to the appropriate class


2. Click "Students"

 


3. Find the student you need to move and click the "delivery truck" icon under the "Actions" column


4. Find and select the correct section to move the student to

 


5. Click "Yes, move the student!". The student has now been moved to the new section.
