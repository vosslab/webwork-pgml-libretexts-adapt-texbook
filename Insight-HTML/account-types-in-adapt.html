Account Types in ADAPT

Last updated at 11/26/2025 02:13 AM by Jake Turner

Description: Accounts types

ADAPT currently has three account types: (1) instructors, (2) graders, and (3) students. Below outlines how these accounts are created.

Instructor Accounts 

Main Features:

  • Make courses and assignments
  • Add assessments to assignments
  • Set and edit course preferences
  • Grade assignments
  • Add Graders and give access to assignments

How to access: Create and instructor account and wait for verification.

Creating Instructor Account

  1. Go to https://register.libretexts.org and create your LibreOne account. Please use your campus email address whenever possible (i.e. avoid @gmail, @yahoo addresses)
  2. Select the "Instructor" role during registration
  3. Once you've finished registration, you'll be automatically prompted to submit your instructor verification request.
  4. Wait for a response from the LibreTexts team regarding your verification request. These are usually processed quite quickly, but please allow up to 48 hours. For more info, please see FAQ: Instructor Verification
  5. Once approved, you can visit https://adapt.libretexts.org and sign in with your LibreOne account. You now have full instructor access to ADAPT!

Grader Accounts 

Grader accounts are designed to streamline open-ended grading. These accounts are designed to handle the grading of questions that autograding technologies are unable to address and require human review. Main features of this account is the ability to

  • grade assignments they are given access to
  • provide students with feedback
  • Answer students questions when contacted via ADAPT

Graders cannot make or edit assignments. Only instructor accounts have that ability.

How to access: Direct invitation from the instructor via ADAPT

Creating Grader Accounts

Instructors Instructions 

  1. Open Course PreferencesFigure 9.1.1
  2. 9.1.1: Use the gear icon to access Course Properties
  3. Select "Graders" in the menu on the left
  4. Click "Invite Grader"Figure 9.1.2
  5. 9.1.2: Select "Graders" in the menu on the left and then click "Invite Grader"
  6. Enter Grader’s email address
  7. Choose sections to give Grader access to or click "select all" to give access to all sections 
  8. Click "Submit". Invited Grader will receive an email with the access code to join. 

Graders Instructions

 

  1. Open your invitation sent via email and copy access codeFigure 9.1.3
  2. 9.1.3: Copy the access code from the email
  3. Go to https://adapt.libretexts.org
  4. Click "Register" in the top right corner
  5. Select "Grader" from the drop-down menu
  6. Note: if you already have a student or instructor account you will need to make a separate account to be a grader using another email
  7. Fill out your information to make your account
  8. After your account is created you will be brought to My Courses page
  9. Click "Add Course"Figure 9.1.4
  10. 9.1.4: Click "Add Course" and paste the access code
  11. Paste the Access Code you received via email
  12. Click Submit

Grader Permissions  

After the grader has made their account and registered, you can set permissions for which assignments they can access.

Under Course Properties select “Grader Permissions”. You can give grading access to individual assignments and graders, or you can select "give all graders access" to give all graders access to all assignments

Figure 9.1.5

9.1.5: Use "Grader Permissions" to select which grader will have access to which assignments or give all graders access to all assignments

Student Account 

Main Features:

  • Join Courses
  • View Assignments
  • Submit Answers
  • View grader's feedback
  • Contact Grader

How to access: Access code created by the instructor.

Creating Student Accounts

Instructors Instructions

  1. Open Course PreferencesFigure 9.1.6
  2. 9.1.6: Use the gear icon to access Course Properties
  3. Select "Sections" in the menu on the left
  4. Copy access code.
  5. Note: each section will have a different access codeFigure 9.1.7
  6. 9.1.7: Copy the access code
  7. Give this access code to the students

Students Instructions 

  1. Make sure you have an access code from your instructor. Each course and each section will have a different access code. 
  2. Go to https://adapt.libretexts.org
  3. Click Register in the top right corner
  4. Select "Student" from the drop-down menu
  5. Fill out your information to make your account
  6. After your account is created you will be brought to My Courses page. 
  7. Click "Enroll in Course"

  9. Figure 9.1.8
  10. 9.1.8: Click "Enroll in Course" and paste the access code you received from your instructor
