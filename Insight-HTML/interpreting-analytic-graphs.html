Description: Interpreting Analytic Graphs

EssayMaps present a large-scale view of the entire editing history of the document. In the EssayMap, time is shown on the x-axis (horizontally), and the physical location of the edits in the text is represented on the y-axis (vertically). The y-axis is flipped from a conventional graph so that the beginning of the document is at the top of the graph and that the end of the document is at the bottom – like in a written document. Pasted in language is represented by green or blue circles in the EssayMap – the larger the insertion, the larger the circle. Deleted language is represented by red circles in the EssayMap.
